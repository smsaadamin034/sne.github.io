Project Overview:

This project involves the comprehensive design and installation of Low and Medium Voltage Electrical Systems for a Gas Plant in the Oil and Gas sector. The scope of work included designing, coordinating, and implementing electrical systems to meet the specific needs and challenges of the gas plant environment.

Acknowledgment:

I extend my gratitude to Schneider Electric Pakistan for the opportunity and support provided during this project, which I was responsible for while working at Schneider Electric Pakistan.

Low Voltage (LV) and Medium Voltage (MV) System Layout:

The detailed layout plan includes well-organized rows of panels to ensure efficient space utilization and optimal cable routing. Each row is clearly labeled for easy identification and installation.

LOW Voltage (LV) Panels:

Incoming (Trafo)/ Incoming (Gen Set)/Outgoing Feeder Panels, Bus Coupler,MCC (Fixed/Drawout Type Starter), VFD and Soft Starter Panels and Dummy Panel.

BTD (Bus Trunking Duct) and Overhead BTD (Bus Trunking Duct)

BTD (Bus Trunking Duct): Details on the bus trunking systems used for efficient electrical distribution.

Overhead BTD: Specifications and benefits of overhead bus trunking systems.

Medium Voltage (MV) Panels:

Incoming Panel

Transformer Panels

Bus Couplers

MCC Panels

Bus Measuring/Earthing

Bus Riser/Adapter

Project Documentation

The project encompasses comprehensive engineering documentation, which includes:

1. Schematics: Visual representations of the electrical systems for clarity.

2.Truth Table for Interlocking: A detailed truth table used for interlocking operations, ensuring safe and correct operation sequences.

3. Interconnection Diagrams: Detailed connections between different components.

4. Looping Diagrams: Closed-loop systems for monitoring and control.

5. Cable Schedules: Specifications and layouts for cable management.

6. Signal Lists: Comprehensive lists of all input and output signals for system integration.

7. Foundation Plans: Detailed layouts showing structural support, stability, and cable entry points, including provisions for power and control cable entry to ensure organized and efficient installation.
