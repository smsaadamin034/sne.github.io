Comprehensive Electrical Support

Providing full-spectrum electrical support services, including on-site installation, inventory management, ERP integration, and specialized services, all delivered in compliance with IEC/NEMA standards.

Detailed Services:

  • Project Management & Coordination: Overseeing all aspects of electrical project implementation, ensuring timelines and budgets are met, while adhering to IEC/NEMA standards.
  • Installation & Supervision: Providing expert installation and supervision services to ensure systems are installed correctly and operate efficiently.
  • Post-Installation Maintenance: Offering ongoing maintenance and support to ensure systems continue to function at peak efficiency, with regular updates as per IEC/NEMA guidelines.
  • ERP Integration for Electrical Systems: Integrating electrical systems with ERP platforms to ensure seamless operation and management of inventory, procurement, and other key processes.
