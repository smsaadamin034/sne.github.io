Detailed High Voltage System Design:

This service focuses on the comprehensive design and engineering of high-voltage systems, ensuring reliable and safe operation in compliance with international standards.

  • Overall Protection Concept Diagram:

    • Develop a comprehensive protection concept diagram for the high-voltage system.

    Concept Development: Formulated an overall protection strategy tailored to the specific needs of high-voltage systems, ensuring system integrity and safety.

  • Component Selection:

    • Select the most suitable components for high-voltage protection and control panels.

    Component Evaluation: Carefully selected components that offer optimal performance and reliability, adhering to IEC 61850 and NEMA 250 standards.

  • Relay Lists:

    • Compile detailed and accurate relay lists for high-voltage systems.

    Relay Documentation: Created comprehensive lists of relays, specifying their functions and configurations to ensure seamless operation and protection.

  • Concept Diagram, Trip Logic, Relay List & Signal List:

    • Prepare detailed design documents for high-voltage protection systems.

    Developed detailed concept diagrams, trip logic, relay lists, and signal lists, ensuring a clear understanding of system operations and fault management.

  • Schematics and Interlocking Logics:

    • Design and implement interlocking logics to ensure safe operation of high-voltage systems.

    Schematic Development: Created detailed schematics and interlocking logics for the control and protection of high-voltage substations, including Medium Voltage panels, following IEC 60947 and NEMA ICS 1 standards.

  • CT/VT Calculation:

    • Perform precise technical calculations for Current Transformers (CT) and Voltage Transformers (VT).

    Transformer Sizing: Conducted technical calculations to determine the appropriate sizes and ratings of CTs and VTs, ensuring accurate measurement and protection, in compliance with IEC 60044 and NEMA PT 1 standards.

  • Cable Schedule Preparation:

    • Prepare detailed cable schedules for high-voltage systems.

    Cable Documentation: Developed comprehensive cable schedules for interfacing control and protection panels from the substation, ensuring proper cable management and installation, adhering to IEC 60228 and NEMA WC 70 guidelines.

  • Termination Plan:

    • Develop precise plans for cable termination in high-voltage systems.

    Designed and implemented detailed termination plans to ensure secure and reliable connections, following best practices and industry standards.

Our high-voltage system designs ensure maximum reliability, safety, and efficiency, meeting international standards at every stage of the process.
