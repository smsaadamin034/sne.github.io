On-Grid Solar System Design Training

Conducted at Exide Pakistan Limited | June - July 2023

This specialized training focused on equipping participants with the knowledge and skills needed for efficient and effective design and implementation of On-Grid Solar Systems. Over the course of two months, key areas were covered in-depth, including:

AC/DC Cable & Losses Calculation: Detailed analysis of cable losses to optimize power transmission and ensure efficiency.

Voltage Drop Calculation: Techniques to maintain proper electrical distribution by accurately calculating and mitigating voltage drops.

AC/DC Circuit Breaker Selection & Calculation: Guidance on selecting and sizing circuit breakers based on system requirements and standards.

Cable Management: Structured approaches to designing cable trays and trenches for organized and safe cable routing.

Switchgear Design: Selection and design of DC/AC switchgear, focusing on IP class and form protection standards to meet system demands.

Busbar Calculation: Techniques for calculating effective power distribution within the system, ensuring balanced load sharing and system stability.

Short Circuit Calculations: Methods to evaluate system stability and protection, with a focus on short circuit scenarios.

Transformer Selection: Selecting the appropriate transformers based on application-specific needs, ensuring reliable and efficient operation.

Medium Voltage Selection: Advanced techniques for selecting medium voltage equipment to enhance system integration and overall performance.

Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical exercises, participants gained the expertise needed to analyze electrical systems critically and make informed design decisions. This training played a vital role in empowering professionals to contribute effectively to On-Grid Solar System projects, preparing them to excel in their careers within the renewable energy sector.
