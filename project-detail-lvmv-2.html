Project Overview:

This project involves the design and installation of various types of distribution boards suitable for different environments, including residential, industrial, healthcare, and shopping malls. The boards are designed for both indoor and outdoor use, with IP ratings ranging from IP40 to IP65 to ensure durability and safety.

Acknowledgment:

This work was carried out while I was working at Areva T&D Pakistan, before it became Schneider Electric.

Types of Distribution Boards

Flush Type: Residential and commercial buildings.Installed flush with the wall for a neat look.

Surface Wall Mounted: Ideal for industrial and commercial use.Mounted on the wall for easy access.

Floor Standing Board: Designed for industrial and large commercial spaces.Free-standing, robust, and easy to maintain.

Features

Indoor and Outdoor Use: Withstands various environmental conditions.

IP Class 40 to IP65: Protection ranging from dust to water.

Durability and Safety: Ensures long-lasting performance and user safety.
